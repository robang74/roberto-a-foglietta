Jony Ive's AI hardware startup is named io. Founded in 2024 by Ive alongside

objects, and managing complex tasks—into a more ambient and less attention-
demanding experience.

for both OpenAI and io, ensuring a focus on human-centric design in the
development of these innovative AI devices.

As of now, io, the AI hardware startup co-founded by Jony Ive, does not appear
to have a public-facing website. The company has operated in stealth mode,

interaction with technology.

In May 2025, OpenAI announced the acquisition of io in a $6.5 billion all-
equity deal, marking its significant expansion into consumer hardware. The
collaboration between Jony Ive and OpenAI CEO Sam Altman aims to create a new
category of AI-native hardware that offers an alternative to traditional
smartphones.

While specific details about the devices are still under wraps, the envisioned
products are expected to integrate advanced AI functionalities—such as
summarizing information, recognizing objects, and managing complex tasks—into a
more ambient and less attention-demanding experience. The first product from
this collaboration is anticipated to launch in 2026.

Jony Ive's design firm, LoveFrom, will continue to lead the creative direction
for both OpenAI and io, ensuring a focus on human-centric design in the
development of these innovative AI devices.
