The Microsoft fortune

Returning to the chart, the best three products that are on the top list of revenue inflow are XBOX, Linkedin and GPT. None of them has been developed by Microsoft but they just bought. So, if the money bought the Microsoft owned best products, where did the Microsoft money come from?

Some people think that they became rich when they stole the NEXT's windows-based OS and they sell it as their own product. This can prove that even Windows OS has not been made by them but is wrong thinking that it is the reason for their fortune. Doing a step back, the MS-DOS was a worse alternative to the IBM DR-DOS but the DR-DOS floppy disks set was protected by the copy. Few people were able to copy it, despite protection.

Copying is fundamental to spread around a software. Because a license is not only a cost but a time delay, especially before Internet. Windows inherits the same goodness: easy to copy, hence easy to spread. When Windows became the standard de-facto about personal computing, the US Congress made a law and a huge lobbying operation that enforced all WTO countries contrasting copying as piracy and piracy as felony.

Easy-to-copy and the US gov support made Microsoft guys rich. This is the main reason because they are a subsidiary of the US gov. In the good and in the bad.
